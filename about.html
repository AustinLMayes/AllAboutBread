Humble Beginnings

All About Bread was founded on January 9, 1998 by a group of local bread enthusiasts who wanted to spread the wonders of bread to the rest of the world. This was during the time of the dot-com bubble, so the logical first step was to create a website. The site was simple at first, but grew to what it is today due to a hard working team of developers and from new innovations in browser technology.


The Future

Over these wonderful 20 years our small community has grown into an extremely active one with 1,000 daily active users and over 1 million unique page views per month. The next 20 years should be even brighter! We are working on new and exciting projects each and every day in an effort to make this great community even greater. We are listening to your suggestions and are actively making improvements to the website and planning more events where we can all meet up and share a slice.
