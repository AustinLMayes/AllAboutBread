Welcome to All About Bread, the site created for, and by, bread lovers around the world. Here at AAB our mission is to spread the word about all of the wonderful types of bread, and to expand palettes across the globe to include more than just plain old wheat bread.



This site has many features, including:

Community
We have a community of over 10,000 members who actively contribute to the growing library of recipes and bread articles.
Recipes
Want to learn how to make the wonderful bread types shown on the site in your own home? Each bread types contains a detailed recipe which will allow you to enjoy a loaf from the comfort of your own couch.
Workshops
We hold workshops every month in major cities across the US in an effort to enhance the baking industry and to help teach new members some general baking techniques.
Competition
Every year our members compete in a nation-wide baking competition to see who can make the most unique form of a specific type of bread. These competitions attract crowds in the thousands and have rewards of up to $10,000.
